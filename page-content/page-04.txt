Page 4: Security & SPV Ownership

Badge: SPV Ownership & DIFC Regulation

Headline: You Own Real Property. Not Promises. Not IOUs. Actual Registered Ownership.

Answer Capsule:
You've seen too many investment 'opportunities' that turned out to be smoke and mirrors. This isn't one of them. When you invest with InvestDubai, you hold equity shares in a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) registered in the DIFC, which owns the villa's title deed. This isn't a promise, IOU, or token—it's registered property ownership under English common law, one of the world's strongest legal frameworks. Even if our company disappeared tomorrow, your ownership of the underlying villa remains legally protected. The SPV is your firewall.

How You're Protected:

1. You Own Shares in a Dedicated Legal Entity — Each villa is owned by a dedicated SPV. You own equity shares in this SPV, making you a real co-owner with your name on legal documents.

2. Your SPV Owns the Title Deed — The SPV holds the title deed to the villa, registered with the Dubai Land Department.

3. You're Protected by English Common Law — Your SPV is registered in the DIFC, operating under English common law.

4. Your Assets Are Firewall-Protected — Your investment in each project is legally separate from our company operations.

5. You're in a Regulated Environment — All investors go through verified identity checks (KYC/KYB).

6. You're Protected from Budget Overruns — Renovation budgets are locked in upfront. Any overruns are absorbed by us.
