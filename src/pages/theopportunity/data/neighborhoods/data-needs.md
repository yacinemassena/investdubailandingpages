## Update Schedule

These data points should be reviewed and updated:
- **Quarterly**: Price ranges and rental yields

## Notes

- All price data in articles uses AED (UAE Dirhams)
- Yield calculations assume gross yields unless otherwise specified
- Service charge data should be verified with building management
- Transaction data from DLD is the most reliable source for actual sales prices
